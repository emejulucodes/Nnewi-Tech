FAQ


  • How can someone register?

    By clicking here

  • What's the duration?

    3 months for the basic class. When done with basic, you can register for the advanced class.

  • How much?
    • Website Development FullStack - 50K.
    • Mobile App Development - 70K.
    • UI/UX Design - 50K.
    • Data Science - 100K.
  • Must I pay everything once?

    No. You can spread payment for 3 months. You can even start without payment but must make a payment within one month.

  • Location?

    3rd Floor, Nnewi Digital Village, No 1 St Peters Clavers Rd Besides Next Level Lounge off Old Onitsha Rd, Nnewichi Nnewi.

  • I don't have a laptop.

    You can start. A computer will be provided or shared with you.

  • Class Schedule?

    Tuesday to Saturday. 10am to 4pm.
